Title: Sai An Ju Ma Au
Artist: Trio Ambisi
Artist 2: [Victor Hutabarat]
Note: There are two different artists that worked on this music, however they share a little bit of differences. The one made by Trio Ambisi is the original song and artist, whereas Victor Hutabarat is someone who covers the music titled "Sai Anju Ma Au"  technically meaning "Persuade Me" but in a romantic way.
